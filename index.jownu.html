Bitfinex Publishes a Plan for the Exchange's Revival

Cryptocurrency exchange Bitfinex plans to gradually recover access for users in the wake of a hack and theft of 120k BTC. The exchange's administration has reiterated its statement that the team is working on reviving all functions. Currently, users may check their balance, however, trading, withdrawal and depositing are not available as for now.
