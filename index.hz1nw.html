Belgium Regulators Issue Warning About OneCoin Investment Scheme - CoinDesk

A top financial regulator in Belgium has issued a warning about OneCoin, a digital currency product that has frequently drawn fraud accusations. The notice, published this weekend, states that remarks from promoters that OneCoin has received the institution's tacit approval are "false and misleading".
