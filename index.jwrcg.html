Bitcoin customers lose 36% of their money after hack

Hong Kong-based Bitfinex said it's working to reopen the exchange by Monday. The platform was shut down on August 3 after 119,756 bitcoins, worth more than $65 million based on exchange rates at the time, were stolen from some users' accounts. Bitfinex said it decided "after much thought, analysis and consultations" to generalize the losses across all users.

To compensate its customers, Bitfinex said users will receive tokens that may later be redeemed or exchanged for shares in its parent company.
